UQ Mathematics Student Society

MSS is the UQ Mathematics Student Society, run by students to promote and improve the experience for math students at UQ. We run a range of events including math talks, industry talks and social events.

Sponsors

MSS is proudly sponsored by a range of industry sponsors. These sponsors provide access to a range of opportunities for our members, as well as the ability to hear directly from sponsors at our events.
